Budget Travel Notes (India)

Typical Daily Costs (mid-range):
- Lodging: INR 1200-3000
- Food: INR 500-1200
- Local transport: INR 300-700

Savings Tips:
- Use trains or buses for intercity travel when possible.
- Eat at local cafes and markets for lower prices.
- Avoid peak holiday weekends to reduce hotel costs.
