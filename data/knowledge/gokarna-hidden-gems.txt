Gokarna Hidden Gems

Om Beach: sunrise walks and short treks.
Half Moon Beach: boat access, quieter than main beaches.
Kudle Beach: cafes and laid-back vibe.
Nearby:
Mirjan Fort: late-afternoon visit for cooler temperatures.
Yana Caves: day trip, carry water and sturdy shoes.

Budget Notes:
Local guesthouses are cheaper on weekdays.
Shared auto rides cut short-distance costs.
