Goa Overview

Best Season:
Nov-Feb: pleasant weather, peak demand.
Mar-May: hot, fewer crowds.
Jun-Sep: monsoon; beach activities limited.

Neighborhoods:
North Goa: nightlife, busy beaches, markets.
South Goa: quieter, family-friendly, calmer beaches.

Transport Tips:
Scooter rentals are common; check license and helmet rules.
Taxis can be pricey; pre-book for long trips.
